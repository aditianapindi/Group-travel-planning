# Key Research Questions

## User Segments
1. Who are the core user segments? (Friend groups, families, couples, corporate?)
2. What distinguishes the "organizer" persona from "participant" personas?
3. How do planning behaviors differ across segments (weekend trip vs. 2-week international)?

## Pain Points
4. What are the highest-friction moments in group planning today?
5. Where do people abandon or compromise on group trips?
6. What emotional toll does coordination take on organizers?

## Competitive Landscape
7. Where do existing tools (WhatsApp groups, Google Sheets, Splitwise, TripIt) fail?
8. What do competitors get right that we should not rebuild?
9. Why haven't existing solutions won this market?

## Product Strategy
10. What's the minimum feature set that delivers 10x improvement over group chats?
11. What's the business model that aligns with user value?
12. What's the wedge — which segment do we win first?
