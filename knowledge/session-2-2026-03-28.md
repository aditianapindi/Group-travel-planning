## Key insights surfaced
- Highest pain: friend groups planning international trips (4-6 people)
- Biggest white space: multigenerational families (8-20 people) — no app attempts this
- Tension: highest-pain ≠ biggest white space. Build for friend groups first, design data model for families.
- Core product insight: risk of building logistics tool when the real need is emotional momentum through the death zone
- Obligated travelers (family, weddings) behave differently than excited travelers (friend trips) — interviews must distinguish these
