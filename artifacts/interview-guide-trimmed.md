# Group Travel Planning — Interview Guide (Trimmed)
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes | **Format:** Casual conversation (phone/video)
**14 questions — focused on what 10+ interviews haven't yet answered**

---

## Opening (2 min)

1. Tell me about the last group trip you planned or were part of. How many people? Where did you go? Were you the organizer?

---

## The Story (8 min)

2. Walk me through how the trip came together — from the first "let's go somewhere" to actually leaving. What happened step by step?
   *(Let them talk. Listen for: tools used, where it stalled, who did the work, how long it took. Don't interrupt — most of your other answers will come from this.)*

3. Was there a moment where the trip almost didn't happen? What caused that?

---

## Targeted Probes (5 min — pick 2-3 based on conversation)

4. Did anyone say their budget out loud before planning started — or did you just figure it out as you went? What happened when expectations didn't match?
   *(Use only if budget didn't come up naturally in Q2)*

5. Were there people who didn't participate in planning at all? What happened when they showed up on the trip — were they happy with the plan?
   *(Tests the Vinay problem: silent in planning, frustrated on trip)*

6. Was there a point where planning just stalled — weeks of silence? What killed the momentum, and what revived it?
   *(Tests death zone — only if they mentioned a long planning timeline)*

---

## Business Viability (5 min — these are the questions you don't have answers to yet)

7. Have you ever downloaded an app specifically for trip planning? What happened — did you use it for the next trip, or go back to WhatsApp?
   *(Tests: does this category retain users at all?)*

8. Be honest — if your organizer friend said "download this app for our next trip," how many people in your group would actually do it? What's the minimum it would need to do?
   *(Tests adoption wall — the #1 category killer)*

9. What if the tool worked inside WhatsApp — like a bot or link in the group chat — instead of being a separate app? Would that change anything?
   *(Tests WhatsApp-native thesis)*

10. When you're ready to book, do you book where you planned, or go to a site you already trust like Booking.com or MakeMyTrip?
   *(Tests booking leakage — can you monetize through bookings?)*

11. How many group trips do you take in a year? How many different groups have you planned for in the past 2 years?
   *(Tests frequency AND cross-group organizing. If the same person plans for family + friends + milestone trips, they're a serial organizer with 2-3x the frequency. No published data exists on this — we need to validate.)*

12. Would your group pay $3-5 total per trip for a tool that saved the organizer 10+ hours of work? Or does it need to be free?
   *(Direct WTP test)*

---

## Planning Trigger & Timing (2 min)

13. When you decide to plan a trip, what triggers it? How far ahead do you start? Has a trip ever died because planning started too late — or dragged on too long?
   *(Tests planning lead time and the "death zone." Secondary research shows 3-6 month windows = highest stress. Also tests whether there's a festival/holiday trigger pattern — especially relevant for India.)*

---

## Close (1 min)

14. What's the one thing that would make group trip planning 10x easier for you?
